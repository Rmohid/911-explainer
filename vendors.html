Overview of NG911 Vendors

The NG911 ecosystem includes a variety of vendors offering solutions for call handling, routing, CAD, radio integration, analytics, and more. Some focus on specific components, while others provide end-to-end PSAP solutions.

Avaya

  • Key Products: Avaya Aura Communication Manager, Application Enablement Services (AES), DMCC API, call handling platforms.
  • Strengths: Deep expertise in telephony, device/media control, enterprise-grade call management, integration with existing PBX systems.
  • Implementation: Typically on-premises deployments, tightly integrated with Avaya hardware/software. Supports SIP trunking, DMCC for device/media control, and CTI integrations.
  • Support: Extensive enterprise support network, large partner ecosystem, proven reliability in large-scale deployments.

Motorola Solutions

  • Key Products: VESTA 911 call handling, CommandCentral software suite, CAD systems, radio dispatch integration.
  • Strengths: End-to-end PSAP solutions including call handling, CAD, radio, and analytics. Strong integration with Motorola radios and P25 systems. Cloud-based options available.
  • Implementation: Mix of on-premises and cloud deployments. Emphasis on seamless integration between call handling, dispatch, and radio communications.
  • Support: Focused on public safety, nationwide support teams, extensive experience with PSAP operations.

Other Notable Vendors

  • Intrado: ESInet services, NGCS, call routing, location services.
  • RapidSOS: Data integration platform providing rich caller data to PSAPs.
  • Hexagon: CAD and dispatch software, analytics.
  • Zetron: Radio dispatch consoles, integrated call handling.
  • NICE: Recording, analytics, quality assurance.
  • Carbyne: Cloud-native call handling and video integration.

Comparison of Vendor Approaches

  • Telephony vs. End-to-End: Avaya specializes in telephony and device control, while Motorola offers full PSAP suites including CAD and radio.
  • Deployment Models: Avaya is primarily on-premises; Motorola offers both on-prem and cloud options.
  • Radio Integration: Motorola has deep integration with P25 and LMR systems; Avaya relies on third-party integrations.
  • Data and Analytics: RapidSOS, NICE, and Hexagon provide enhanced data and analytics capabilities.
  • Support: All major vendors offer enterprise/public safety support, but focus areas differ (enterprise vs. public safety).

Conclusion

The NG911 vendor landscape is diverse, with options ranging from specialized telephony solutions to comprehensive PSAP platforms. Choosing the right vendor depends on existing infrastructure, desired features, deployment preferences, and support needs.
